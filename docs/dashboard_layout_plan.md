# Dashboard Layout Plan

## Theme

Professional dark executive dashboard.

- Background: `#0B0F14`
- Panel background: `#111827`
- Card background: `#151C26`
- Primary accent: `#00E5FF`
- Growth accent: `#34D399`
- Warning accent: `#F59E0B`
- Text primary: `#F8FAFC`
- Text secondary: `#94A3B8`

## Page 1: Executive Overview

Top row:

- Total EV Sales KPI
- Market Growth % KPI
- Top Manufacturer KPI
- Top Country KPI
- Average EV Price KPI
- Charging Station Coverage KPI

Main section:

- Left: Global map of EV adoption by country.
- Center: EV sales trend over time with BEV/PHEV split.
- Right: Market share by manufacturer.

Bottom section:

- Country-wise growth ranking.
- Charging coverage vs EV stock scatterplot.
- Executive insight text box powered by selected market DAX.

## Page 2: Market Growth Intelligence

Visuals:

- Country YoY growth heatmap.
- CAGR by country bar chart.
- EV sales share by year line chart.
- BEV vs PHEV trend small multiples.
- Top growth markets table.

## Page 3: Manufacturer & Model Performance

Visuals:

- Manufacturer market share donut or treemap.
- Manufacturer ranking bar chart.
- Revenue contribution by manufacturer.
- Average MSRP by manufacturer.
- Model-level table with range, MSRP, count, and rank.

## Page 4: Battery & Vehicle Technology

Visuals:

- Battery technology comparison matrix.
- Electric range distribution histogram.
- Price-to-range scatterplot.
- BEV/PHEV mix by model year.
- Top models by range.

## Page 5: Charging Infrastructure

Visuals:

- Charging points by country.
- Charging coverage per 1,000 EVs.
- Fast vs slow charger split.
- Infrastructure gap by country.
- Charging readiness score card.

## Page 6: Forecasting & Scenario Analysis

Visuals:

- Forecasted EV sales through 2030.
- Scenario comparison.
- Forecast growth %.
- Historical vs forecast trend.
- CAGR benchmark line.

## UX Details

- Persistent top navigation bar.
- Reset filters button on every page.
- Page-level slicers for year, country, region, powertrain, and scenario.
- Tooltip pages for country, manufacturer, and charging metrics.
- Drill-through enabled on country and manufacturer visuals.
